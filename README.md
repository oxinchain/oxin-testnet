## 📖 About Oxin Chain
Oxin Chain is a next-generation blockchain designed for high scalability, security, and true decentralization. Our mission is to provide a robust infrastructure for developers and a seamless experience for users, empowering the future of Web3, decentralized applications (dApps), and smart contracts.

Learn more about our vision and **Tokenomics** on our official website: 
👉 **[oxinchain.io](https://oxinchain.io)**

## 🧪 About The Testnet
Welcome to the Oxin Testnet! This phase is crucial for testing network stability, smart contract deployment, and validator performance before our official Mainnet launch. We invite developers, node operators, and crypto enthusiasts to participate, stress-test the network, and help us build a resilient ecosystem.
