### 🚀 NETWORK HIGHLIGHTS (For Users & Validators)
* **Deflationary Model:** Features a **2% Burn Mechanism** on transaction base fees (EIP-1559).
* **Validator Rewards:** Validators earn **0.0035 OXIN** per block + transaction tips.
* **Staking Requirement:** A minimum stake of **1,000 OXIN** is required to secure the network and earn rewards.
* **Halving Schedule:** Block rewards will be reduced by **50% every 4 years** to ensure long-term scarcity and value growth.
